All the beauty of Ireland in one girl ! !

She comes from Kilkenny, Ireland, Brianna Blade, the knife thrower will amaze you! She is gorgeous, she's smart ... and she's skilled!

Throwing knives at people wasn't not enough, now she does it, but after a few drinks of Irish whiskey. Each new representation, our Brianna drinks a bottle of alcohol again, before launching her blades.

She must drink 8 bottles of good Irish whiskey, and then choose a person in the room (maybe you?*) for high performance precision!
